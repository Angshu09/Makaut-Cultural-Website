Our Events

Akimatsuri

Akimasturi is An Anime Fest which is Organised by the students of Anime Club, MAKAUT. First time this fest is executed in 2K23 in the Very First Day of Anweshan Fest

Antoheen

Antoheen is a Farewell Ceremony Organised by the students of Information science and technology students of Makaut University to give Farewell to their respected and beloved seniors.

Ajantrik

Ajantrik is a Fresher's Party Organised by the Information science and technology students of Makaut University to welcome the Freshmate students in BCA & BSC Department

Rabindra Jayanti

Celebrating Rabindra Jayanti, a day to immerse ourselves in the beauty of Tagore's creations and pay homage to his artistic brilliance.

GDSC Event

For the very first time, Makaut Launching Google Developer Students Club in 2k23 for the Groups of Thinker, Problem-Solvers and Development Students of Information Technology and Computer Science also non- technology department.

Anweshan 2023

Anweshan is a Tech and Cultural Fest, which is organised in Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, West Bengal. In MAKAUT, It is Considered as a Biggest Fest of the Year Where the students of affiliated colleges also participate in this fest.
